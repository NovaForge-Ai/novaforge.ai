A Step Towards Automated Interactions

Our School ERP System leverages Whatsapp as primary communication tool to automate interactions between School Administration, Faculty, Staff, Students and Parents in a accessible and hassle-free way

Automated Admissions and Enrollment Process

As the new academic session begins, the registration, screening, and admission processes can be daunting for both the administration, staff, and parents alike. NovaSkool ERP System simplifies this by automating announcements, registration forms, and online screening tests. Our AI-powered WhatsApp chatbot facilitates personalized interaction between students, parents, and the school administration, providing round-the-clock assistance in resolving queries.

Online Fee Collection and Management System

NovaSkool eliminates the manual hassle of fee collection and management, from notifying guardians to physically submitting receipts. Our Online Payment Gateway sends personalized alerts to parents regarding fee dues, fee structures, and payment deadlines. Parents can conveniently pay fees via WhatsApp, and transactions are updated in real-time in the Administration Fee Collection Portal with digital receipts. The Payments Dashboard enables the administration to verify transactions for each student effortlessly.

Student Progress Tracking, Performance and Evaluation Insights

NovaSkool addresses the challenge of guardians unable to visit schools or institutions for student updates. Faculty and parents can conveniently monitor student learning progress and performance anytime, anywhere, through NovaSkool ERP System. Our platform seamlessly integrates with existing Learning Management Systems (LMS), enhancing accessibility. Furthermore, our AI-powered Intelligence Insights Module analyzes student performances, providing personalized recommendations and insights to improve student outcomes and performance.

Academic Planning, Management and Resource Distribution

NovaSkool Academic Module simplifies the academic planning and management by allowing faculty to create and distribute course modules, assignments, separate calender events online for the courses with great ease, enhancing overall teaching and learning experiences. With the ability to set up push notifications through WhatsApp, instructors can effortlessly keep students informed about important updates and deadlines. Additionally, sharing learning resources with the class becomes effortless with just a single click.
